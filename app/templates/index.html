How To Play?

For example, Alice vote 0.01BCH on ODD in Round #563492, Bob vote 0.01BCH on EVEN in Round #563492, if block #563492 contain 19 transactions, then Alice win 0.02BCH, The system will charge only 0.00002 as fee, so Alice will receive 0.01998BCH after two confirmations. If one side less than other side, the winner side only can get at most 2 times of self total amount.

  • Step1. Send Transaction to ODD or EVEN address (minimal 0.0001 BCH)
  • Step2. Waiting for new block to get Tx count (need 2 confirmations)
  • Step3. System send reward to your address (charge 0.00002 BCH as fee)
